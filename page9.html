Service (On site)



Daner Engineering specializes in service, after sales and engineering, process and project management. We offer our knowledge in the refrigerating and boiler industry, primarily in the brewery and soft drink industry, not limited to the CO2 industry.

Regular visits to our customers help to evaluate the need for a local stock of spare parts. We offer to act as a consultant with regard to your overall Equipment, optimizing of production capacity and lower operational cost.

To ensure a geographical proximity to our clients, Daner Engineering has a registered subsidiary company, U2C Global Service in Singapore.
